  1. fried rice

    2. For the ingridents:

    • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably leftover or day-old)
    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, corn)
    • Cooked meat (e.g., chicken, shrimp, pork) diced
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions

    1. 1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
    2. Add diced onion and minced garlic; stir-fry until they're translucent.
    3. Add mixed veggies, cooked meat (if using), curry powder, cumin, cinnamon, and nutmeg; stir-fry for 2-3 minutes
    4. Add cooked rice, salt, and pepper; stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
    5. If using tomato puree or paste, add it and stir-fry for another minute.
    6. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
    7. Serve hot and enjoy!

  3. pancake

    4. ingridents

    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons sugar
    • 2 teaspoons baking powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup milk
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
    • Butter or oil for greasing the pan

    the instructions:

    1. Whisk dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt).
    2. Whisk wet ingredients (milk, egg, melted butter).
    3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stir until just combined.
    4. Rest batter for 5-10 minutes.
    5. Preheat pan, grease with butter or oil.
    6. Scoop batter onto pan, cook for 2-3 minutes.
    7. Flip, cook for another 1-2 minutes.
    8. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
